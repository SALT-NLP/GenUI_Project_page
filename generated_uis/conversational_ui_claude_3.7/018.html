Initial Analysis Recommendations

Without seeing your specific data, here are key analyses I recommend:

Population Analysis

Visit Pattern Analysis

  • No-show rates and factors influencing them
  • Seasonal variations in visit types

Treatment Effectiveness

  • Outcome measurements for common conditions
  • Comparison of treatment protocols
  • Follow-up compliance and its impact on outcomes

Resource Utilization

  • Provider productivity metrics
  • Room utilization rates
  • Supply and medication usage patterns
