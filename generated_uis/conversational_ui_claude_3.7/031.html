I'd be happy to help you learn some common Japanese phrases for your trip! Here are essential Japanese phrases organized by category that will help you navigate daily situations:

Greetings and Basics

  • Konnichiwa (こんにちは) - Hello/Good afternoon
  • Ohayou gozaimasu (おはようございます) - Good morning
  • Konbanwa (こんばんは) - Good evening
  • Arigatou gozaimasu (ありがとうございます) - Thank you very much
  • Sumimasen (すみません) - Excuse me/I'm sorry
  • Onegaishimasu (お願いします) - Please
  • Hai (はい) - Yes
  • Iie (いいえ) - No

Self-Introduction

  • Watashi wa [your name] desu (私は [your name] です) - I am [your name]
  • Hajimemashite (はじめまして) - Nice to meet you
  • Amerikajin desu (アメリカ人です) - I am American (adjust for your nationality)
  • Eigo ga hanasemasu (英語が話せます) - I speak English

Directions and Transportation

  • [Place] wa doko desu ka? ([Place] はどこですか?) - Where is [place]?
  • Eki wa doko desu ka? (駅はどこですか?) - Where is the train station?
  • Toirei wa doko desu ka? (トイレはどこですか?) - Where is the bathroom?
  • Ikura desu ka? (いくらですか?) - How much is it?
  • [Place] ni ikitai desu ([Place] に行きたいです) - I want to go to [place]

Dining

  • Osusume wa nan desu ka? (おすすめは何ですか?) - What do you recommend?
  • Kore o kudasai (これをください) - This please (point at menu item)
  • Oishii desu (おいしいです) - It's delicious
  • Okaikei onegaishimasu (お会計お願いします) - Check please
  • Mizu kudasai (水ください) - Water please

Shopping

  • Ikura desu ka? (いくらですか?) - How much is it?
  • Takai desu (高いです) - It's expensive
  • Yasui desu (安いです) - It's cheap
  • Kore o kudasai (これをください) - I'll take this

Emergency/Help

  • Tasukete kudasai (助けてください) - Please help me
  • Byouin wa doko desu ka? (病院はどこですか?) - Where is the hospital?
  • Keisatsu o yonde kudasai (警察を呼んでください) - Please call the police
  • Eigo o hanasu hito wa imasu ka? (英語を話す人はいますか?) - Is there someone who speaks English?

Cultural Phrases

  • Itadakimasu (いただきます) - Said before eating (similar to "bon appétit")
  • Gochisousama deshita (ごちそうさまでした) - Said after finishing a meal
  • Ojama shimasu (お邪魔します) - Said when entering someone's home
  • Shitsurei shimasu (失礼します) - Excuse me (when leaving)
