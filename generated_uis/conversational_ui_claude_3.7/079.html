Theater Management Website Solution

I understand your situation completely! Moving from paper to digital can feel overwhelming, but it'll make managing your theater much easier and help you reach new audiences. Let's break this down into manageable pieces.

What You Need

Based on your description, you need:

  1. A website showing upcoming plays and events
  2. Online ticket sales capability
  3. Seat selection functionality
  4. Mobile ticket scanning for your volunteer ushers
  5. An affordable solution for a non-profit budget

Recommended Approach

WordPress is actually a great starting point! It's:

  • User-friendly for non-technical people
  • Extremely customizable through plugins
  • Affordable to set up and maintain
  • Well-suited for small organizations

Here's how I recommend moving forward:

Step 1: Website Basics

Start with a simple WordPress site with a theater/events theme. Many are designed specifically for performing arts organizations.

Step 2: Ticketing Solution

Add a ticketing plugin like "The Events Calendar" or "Event Espresso" which can handle:

  • Show listings with dates and times
  • Ticket purchases
  • Seat selection
  • Digital ticket delivery (email)
  • QR codes for tickets that can be scanned

Step 3: Mobile Scanning

Most ticketing plugins offer companion mobile apps for checking in attendees. Volunteers can simply use their smartphones to scan tickets.

Cost Considerations

  • WordPress itself: Free
  • Domain name: ~$15/year
  • Web hosting: ~$5-15/month
  • Ticketing plugin: Free to $200/year depending on features
  • Payment processing fees: Typically 2-3% per transaction

The entire solution could cost as little as $200-300 per year plus payment processing fees, which you can pass along to ticket buyers if necessary.
