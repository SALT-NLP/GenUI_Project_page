That’s exciting! Here’s a list of common Japanese phrases you can use for your trip:

Greetings & Basic Phrases:

  1. こんにちは (Konnichiwa) – Hello / Good afternoon
  2. おはようございます (Ohayou gozaimasu) – Good morning
  3. こんばんは (Konbanwa) – Good evening
  4. さようなら (Sayounara) – Goodbye
  5. ありがとうございます (Arigatou gozaimasu) – Thank you (polite)
  6. すみません (Sumimasen) – Excuse me / I’m sorry
  7. ごめんなさい (Gomen nasai) – I’m sorry (more apologetic)
  8. はい (Hai) – Yes
  9. いいえ (Iie) – No
  10. お願いします (Onegaishimasu) – Please (when requesting something)

Asking for Help:

  1. これをください (Kore o kudasai) – Please give me this.
  2. 英語を話せますか? (Eigo o hanasemasu ka?) – Can you speak English?
  3. 助けてください (Tasukete kudasai) – Please help me.
  4. トイレはどこですか? (Toire wa doko desu ka?) – Where is the bathroom?
  5. これはいくらですか? (Kore wa ikura desu ka?) – How much is this?

Transportation:

  1. 駅はどこですか? (Eki wa doko desu ka?) – Where is the station?
  2. この電車は○○に行きますか? (Kono densha wa ○○ ni ikimasu ka?) – Does this train go to ○○?
  3. バス停はどこですか? (Basutei wa doko desu ka?) – Where is the bus stop?
  4. チケットをください (Chiketto o kudasai) – Please give me a ticket.

Ordering Food/Drinks:

  1. メニューを見せてください (Menyuu o misete kudasai) – Please show me the menu.
  2. お水をください (Omizu o kudasai) – Please give me water.
  3. お勧めは何ですか? (Osusume wa nan desu ka?) – What do you recommend?
  4. これは辛いですか? (Kore wa karai desu ka?) – Is this spicy?

Polite Phrases:

  1. 失礼します (Shitsurei shimasu) – Excuse me (when entering a room or interrupting)
  2. お疲れ様です (Otsukaresama desu) – Thank you for your hard work (common at work)
  3. どうぞ (Douzo) – Please (when offering something)

Emergencies:

  1. 警察を呼んでください (Keisatsu o yonde kudasai) – Please call the police.
  2. 病院はどこですか? (Byouin wa doko desu ka?) – Where is the hospital?
  3. 私は道に迷いました (Watashi wa michi ni mayoimashita) – I’m lost.

Other Helpful Phrases:

  1. 日本語を少し話せます (Nihongo o sukoshi hanasemasu) – I can speak a little Japanese.
  2. もう一度お願いします (Mou ichido onegaishimasu) – Please say it again.
  3. ゆっくり話してください (Yukkuri hanashite kudasai) – Please speak slowly.

Feel free to ask if you want more specific phrases!
