Great! Here are a few fun and engaging ways to practice multiplication tables:

1. Games and Apps

  • Times Tables Rock Stars – Fast-paced and competitive.
  • Kahoot! – Create or join live quizzes.
  • Prodigy Math Game – Math RPG with multiplication practice.
  • Math Bingo – Find apps or printables combining bingo and multiplication.

2. DIY Board Games

Make a board with numbered spaces. Roll a die, move forward, and answer a multiplication question to stay on the space—wrong answer, go back!

3. Flashcard Challenge

Use paper or an app like Quizlet. Time yourself to see how fast you can go.

4. Multiplication War (Card Game)

Each player flips 2 cards. Multiply them. Highest product wins the round.

5. Online Multiplication Races

Try websites like:

  • Multiplication.com
  • CoolMathGames.com

6. Color-by-Number Sheets

Search for multiplication color-by-number pages—solve to unlock the picture.

7. Sing Songs

There are multiplication songs for each table on YouTube (like "Multiplication Mash-Up").
