Voice Control & Projection: Lesson 3

Lesson Overview

In this lesson, you'll learn advanced techniques for voice modulation to add emphasis and emotion to your speeches. These skills will help you create more engaging and impactful presentations.

Key Concepts

  • Varying your pitch for emphasis
  • Using volume changes strategically
  • Controlling your speaking pace
  • Adding emotional color to your voice
  • Eliminating monotone delivery
  • Creating vocal variety for audience engagement

Practice Exercise

Record yourself reading the same sentence with different emotions: excitement, sadness, confidence, and curiosity. Pay attention to how your voice naturally changes to express these emotions.

Sample sentence:

"The decision we make today will impact our future for years to come."
