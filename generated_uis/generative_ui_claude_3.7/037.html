Our Story

Leaf & Brew began as a small family-owned tea room in 2005. Our founder, passionate about the art of tea, traveled extensively throughout Asia to study traditional tea cultivation and preparation methods.

Today, we pride ourselves on offering only the highest quality, ethically sourced teas from small farms and estates around the world. We believe in the transformative power of a perfect cup of tea and are dedicated to sharing that experience with our customers.

Every tea in our collection is carefully selected, properly stored, and freshly packaged to ensure you experience the full spectrum of flavors and aromas that make fine teas so extraordinary.

Black Tea

Darjeeling First Flush

$24.95

Our Darjeeling First Flush is harvested in early spring from the finest tea gardens in the foothills of the Himalayas. This exceptional black tea offers a delicate floral aroma with muscatel grape notes and a fresh, lively character.

Origin Darjeeling, India
Caffeine Medium
Flavor Profile Floral, Muscatel, Light
Harvest Spring 2023
Quantity:
Brewing Instructions +

Water Temperature: 185-195°F (85-90°C)

Steep Time: 3-4 minutes

Quantity: 1 tsp per 8 oz cup

For best results, use filtered water and preheat your teapot or cup. This tea can be steeped multiple times, increasing the steep time slightly with each infusion.

Food Pairings +

This delicate tea pairs beautifully with:

  • Light pastries and scones
  • Cucumber sandwiches
  • Fresh fruit, especially stone fruits
  • Mild cheeses
