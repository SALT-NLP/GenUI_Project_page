Company Policies

Attendance & Time Off

Regular attendance is essential for our operations. Our attendance policy includes:

  • Work Hours: Standard shifts are 8 hours with a 30-minute unpaid lunch break and two 15-minute paid rest breaks.
  • Tardiness: Employees are expected to be at their workstations ready to work at the start of their shift.
  • Absences: Notify your supervisor at least 2 hours before your shift if you will be absent.
  • Paid Time Off (PTO): Full-time employees accrue PTO based on length of service.
  • Sick Leave: Employees receive 40 hours of sick leave annually.
  • Holidays: The company observes 10 paid holidays per year.

Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to maintain professional behavior that reflects our company values:

  • Respect: Treat all colleagues, customers, and visitors with dignity and respect.
  • Integrity: Act honestly and ethically in all business dealings.
  • Safety: Prioritize safety in all actions and decisions.
  • Quality: Commit to producing high-quality products that meet or exceed customer expectations.
  • Teamwork: Collaborate effectively with colleagues across departments.

Violations of this code may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment.

Benefits & Resources

Healthcare Benefits

We offer comprehensive healthcare benefits to eligible employees:

  • Medical Insurance: Multiple plan options with coverage for employees and dependents.
  • Dental Insurance: Coverage for preventive, basic, and major dental services.
  • Vision Insurance: Coverage for eye exams, glasses, and contact lenses.
  • Health Savings Account (HSA): Tax-advantaged savings account for medical expenses.
  • Employee Assistance Program (EAP): Confidential counseling and support services.

New employees become eligible for benefits after completing 60 days of employment.

Open Enrollment

Open enrollment occurs annually in November. During this time, you can make changes to your benefit selections for the following year.

Retirement Plans

Our company offers retirement benefits to help you save for your future:

  • 401(k) Plan: Employees can contribute pre-tax earnings to their retirement account.
  • Company Match: The company matches 50% of employee contributions up to 6% of salary.
  • Vesting Schedule: Company matching contributions vest over a 5-year period.
  • Investment Options: Multiple investment options are available to suit different risk tolerances.
  • Financial Education: Resources and workshops to help you make informed retirement decisions.
