Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase tickets online?

Purchasing tickets online is easy! Simply browse our upcoming plays, select the performance date and time you prefer, choose your seats from the interactive seating chart, and proceed to checkout. You'll receive your tickets via email, which you can print or show on your mobile device at the theater.

Can I select specific seats?

Yes! Our online ticketing system allows you to view the theater seating chart and select your preferred seats. Available seats are clearly marked, and you can see which seats are already taken. This feature ensures you get exactly the seats you want for the best viewing experience.

How do mobile tickets work?

After purchasing tickets online, you'll receive an email with your tickets attached as a PDF, which includes a QR code. You can either print these tickets or simply show them on your mobile device when you arrive at the theater. Our ushers will scan the QR code to validate your tickets and guide you to your seats.

What if I don't have a smartphone?

No problem! You can still purchase tickets online and print them at home. Alternatively, you can call our box office to reserve tickets and pick them up at will-call before the performance. We're committed to making our ticketing system accessible to everyone, regardless of technology preferences.

Are there discounts available?

Yes! We offer discounts for seniors, students, military personnel, and groups of 10 or more. We also have season ticket packages that provide significant savings for those who plan to attend multiple shows throughout the year. Check our website or contact our box office for current discount information.
