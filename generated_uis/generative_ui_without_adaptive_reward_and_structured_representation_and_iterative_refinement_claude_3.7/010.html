Introduction to Piano

Welcome to your piano learning journey! This course is designed to guide you from the very basics to playing your first songs. We'll cover everything from proper posture to understanding music notation and playing simple chords and scales.

Learning piano requires patience and consistent practice. Aim for short, regular practice sessions rather than long, infrequent ones. Find a comfortable place to sit and a quiet environment where you can focus.

In this first lesson, we'll get acquainted with the piano itself and set the stage for effective learning.

Ready to start? Let's move on to the next lesson where we'll focus on setting up your playing position.
