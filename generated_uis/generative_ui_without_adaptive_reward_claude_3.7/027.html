What is a SWOT Analysis?

SWOT Analysis is a strategic planning tool used to identify and analyze the Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats of a business or project. It helps you identify internal and external factors that can impact your success.

  • Strengths: Internal positive attributes that give you an advantage
  • Weaknesses: Internal negative attributes that place you at a disadvantage
  • Opportunities: External positive factors that you can capitalize on
  • Threats: External negative factors that could cause trouble
