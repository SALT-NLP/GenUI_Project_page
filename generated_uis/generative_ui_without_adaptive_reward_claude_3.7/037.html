Our Story

Serenity Tea House was born from a passion for exceptional tea and a desire to share the rich traditions of tea culture with our community.

For over 15 years, we've traveled to remote tea gardens across Asia and beyond, building relationships with skilled tea masters and farmers who share our commitment to quality, sustainability, and craftsmanship.

Each tea in our collection is carefully selected for its exceptional character, ethical sourcing, and the story it tells. We believe that fine tea is not just a beverage, but an experience to be savored and shared.

Our mission is to bring the world's finest teas to your cup, along with the knowledge to appreciate their unique qualities and the cultural heritage they represent.

Silver Needle White Tea

★ ★ ★ ★ ★ (24 reviews)
| In Stock
$24.95

Our Silver Needle white tea is hand-picked in the early spring from the mountains of Fujian Province, China. This exquisite tea features silvery, downy buds that produce a pale, straw-colored infusion with delicate, sweet floral notes and a lingering honey finish.

White tea is the least processed of all tea types, allowing you to experience the tea's natural character in its purest form.

Origin & Cultivation

+

Our Silver Needle is harvested from the Da Bai (Big White) tea cultivar in the misty mountains of Fujian Province, China. The tea is picked for just a few days each spring when the perfect buds have formed. Only the unopened buds are selected, making this one of the most labor-intensive teas to produce.

Brewing Guide

+

Water Temperature: 175°F (80°C)
Leaf Amount: 1.5 teaspoons per cup
Steep Time: 3-5 minutes
Re-steeps: This tea can be steeped 2-3 times, adding 1 minute to each subsequent infusion.

Tasting Notes

+

Aroma: Sweet, floral with hints of fresh hay
Flavor: Delicate, smooth with notes of honeysuckle, melon, and a subtle sweetness
Body: Light and refreshing
Finish: Clean with a lingering honey sweetness
