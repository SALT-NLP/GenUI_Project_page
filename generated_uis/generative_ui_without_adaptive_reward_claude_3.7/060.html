Introduction

Safety Procedures

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

All employees working in manufacturing areas must wear appropriate Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)Specialized clothing or equipment worn for protection against health and safety hazards.

Spanish: Equipo de Protección Personal (EPP)
Vietnamese: Thiết bị Bảo hộ Cá nhân
at all times. This includes:

  • Safety glasses or goggles
  • Steel-toed boots
  • Hearing protection in designated areas
  • Hard hats in designated areas
  • Cut-resistant gloves when handling sharp materials
Warning
Failure to wear required PPE may result in disciplinary action and increases the risk of workplace injuries.

Lockout/Tagout Procedures

Lockout/Tagout (LOTO)Lockout/Tagout (LOTO)Safety procedure used to ensure that dangerous machines are properly shut off and not able to be started up again prior to the completion of maintenance or servicing work.

Spanish: Bloqueo/Etiquetado
Vietnamese: Khóa/Gắn thẻ an toàn
procedures must be followed when servicing or maintaining equipment. This procedure ensures that dangerous machines are properly shut off and prevented from starting up again prior to the completion of maintenance or servicing work.

Only authorized employees who have completed LOTO training may perform these procedures. If you need to service equipment and have not been trained, contact your supervisor immediately.

Company Policies

Attendance and Punctuality

Regular attendance and punctuality are essential for efficient operations. Employees are expected to report to work on time and ready to begin work at their scheduled start time.

If you will be absent or late, you must notify your supervisor at least one hour before your scheduled start time. Excessive absenteeism or tardiness may result in disciplinary action.

Non-Discrimination Policy

Manufacturing Co. is committed to providing equal employment opportunities to all individuals without regard to race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, marital status, sexual orientation, gender identity, genetic information, or any other characteristic protected by law.

Any employee who believes they have been subjected to discrimination should report the incident to their supervisor or Human Resources immediately.

Benefits Information

Health Insurance

Manufacturing Co. offers comprehensive health insurance coverage to all full-time employees. Coverage begins on the first day of the month following 30 days of employment.

The health insurance plan includes medical, dental, and vision coverage. Employees may choose to enroll eligible dependents in the plan for an additional premium.

Retirement Plan

The company offers a 401(k) retirement savings plan with company matching contributions. Employees are eligible to participate after 90 days of employment.

Manufacturing Co. matches 50% of employee contributions up to 6% of your salary. Employees are fully vested in the company match after three years of employment.
