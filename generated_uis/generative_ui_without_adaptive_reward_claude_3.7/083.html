Instagram Strategy

tips_and_updates Platform Best Practices

  • check_circle Post high-quality, visually consistent imagery that showcases your sustainable materials and production processes
  • check_circle Use Instagram Stories to share behind-the-scenes content from your sustainable production facilities
  • check_circle Create educational carousel posts about sustainable fashion practices and materials
  • check_circle Leverage Reels to showcase garment versatility and longevity through styling videos
  • check_circle Engage with sustainability communities through relevant hashtags and collaborations

Content Ideas for Instagram

Material Monday Series

Weekly educational carousel posts highlighting sustainable materials used in your products, their environmental benefits, and how to care for them.

Maker Spotlight

Introduce the skilled artisans who create your garments, sharing their stories, expertise, and commitment to quality craftsmanship.

One Piece, Five Ways

Showcase the versatility of your garments by styling one piece multiple ways, emphasizing longevity and value over disposable fashion.

Fast Fashion Impact

Create comparative infographics showing the environmental impact of fast fashion vs. your sustainable practices (water usage, carbon footprint, etc.).

TikTok Strategy

tips_and_updates Platform Best Practices

  • check_circle Create authentic, educational content with a casual, conversational tone
  • check_circle Use trending sounds and formats while adapting them to sustainability messaging
  • check_circle Share quick, impactful facts about fast fashion's environmental impact
  • check_circle Demonstrate transparency through factory tours and production process videos
  • check_circle Engage with sustainability and fashion TikTok communities through challenges and duets

Content Ideas for TikTok

Fast Fashion Facts

Short, impactful videos sharing shocking statistics about fast fashion's environmental and social impact, contrasted with your sustainable solutions.

From Fiber to Fashion

Time-lapse series showing the complete journey of your garments from raw materials to finished products, highlighting the craftsmanship involved.

Sustainable Wardrobe Challenges

Create challenges that encourage viewers to shop their closet, upcycle items, or create capsule wardrobes using your pieces as anchors.

Greenwashing Detector

Educational series teaching viewers how to spot greenwashing in fashion marketing and what genuine sustainability claims look like.

Blog Strategy

tips_and_updates Platform Best Practices

  • check_circle Create in-depth, well-researched content that establishes your brand as a thought leader in sustainable fashion
  • check_circle Optimize content for SEO using keywords related to sustainable fashion, ethical manufacturing, etc.
  • check_circle Include high-quality images and infographics to illustrate sustainable practices and materials
  • check_circle Link to credible sources when citing sustainability facts and statistics
  • check_circle Create content that answers common questions about sustainable fashion to drive organic search traffic

Content Ideas for Blog

The True Cost Series

In-depth articles breaking down the real cost of producing sustainable garments vs. fast fashion, including environmental, social, and economic factors.

Sustainable Fabric Guide

Comprehensive guides to different sustainable fabrics, their properties, environmental impact, and care instructions to help educate consumers.

Meet Our Makers

Profile series featuring interviews with your production partners, factory workers, and artisans, highlighting fair labor practices and skilled craftsmanship.

Capsule Wardrobe Workshop

Step-by-step guides to building versatile, sustainable wardrobes with fewer, higher-quality pieces that work together seamlessly.

Newsletter Strategy

tips_and_updates Platform Best Practices

  • check_circle Maintain a consistent sending schedule to build anticipation and trust
  • check_circle Personalize content when possible based on customer preferences and purchase history
  • check_circle Balance educational content with promotional material (80/20 rule)
  • check_circle Include exclusive content not available on other platforms to reward subscribers
  • check_circle Use clear, compelling subject lines that highlight the value inside

Content Ideas for Newsletter

Sustainability Spotlight

Monthly deep dives into one aspect of your sustainability practices, from water conservation techniques to ethical labor standards.

Sustainable Wardrobe Care

Seasonal guides on how to care for, repair, and maintain garments to extend their lifespan, reducing consumption and waste.

Impact Reports

Quarterly updates on your brand's environmental and social impact, including progress toward sustainability goals and areas for improvement.

Community Highlights

Feature customers who are making sustainable fashion choices, including how they style and care for your pieces long-term.

record_voice_over Brand Voice Management

Authentic

Transparent, honest, and genuine in all communications. We share real stories and facts without exaggeration.

Educational

Informative without being preachy. We empower through knowledge, making sustainability accessible.

Confident

Assured in our expertise and values, but never arrogant. We stand firmly behind our sustainable practices.

Thoughtful

Considerate and reflective in our approach to sustainability, acknowledging complexities and nuances.

auto_awesome Differentiation from Fast Fashion

Transparency Series

Create a multi-platform series documenting your entire supply chain from raw materials to finished product, highlighting the real people and processes behind your garments.

Cost-Per-Wear Calculator

Develop an interactive tool that helps customers understand the true value of investment pieces by calculating cost-per-wear based on quality and longevity compared to fast fashion alternatives.

Longevity Guarantee

Highlight your repair services and durability guarantees, showcasing customer stories of garments still in use after several years with before/after photos.

Environmental Impact Comparison

Create visually compelling infographics comparing the environmental footprint of your production methods versus fast fashion (water usage, carbon emissions, waste generation).

Slow Fashion Challenge

Launch a 30-day challenge encouraging followers to embrace slow fashion principles, with daily tips, styling ideas for existing wardrobes, and repair/upcycling tutorials.

Worker Spotlight

Feature the skilled artisans and factory workers who make your clothes, sharing their expertise, fair compensation, and safe working conditions in contrast to fast fashion labor practices.

Material Deep Dives

Create educational content about your sustainable materials, explaining why they cost more but deliver better quality, comfort, and environmental benefits than synthetic fast fashion fabrics.

Capsule Wardrobe Guides

Develop seasonal guides showing how a few quality pieces can create dozens of outfits, emphasizing versatility and timeless design over trend-chasing disposable fashion.
